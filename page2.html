Waste Free Movement

One Goal. One Act. One Future.

    One of the best part of the world is the nature itself. From rising of the sun to the appearance of the moon. Earth’s surface mostly consists of bodies of water approximately to 71%. Every bodies of water has its own breathtaking characteristics. Oceans with diverse kind of fish and coral reefs, cold & hot breeze, a hydroelectricity produced from waterfalls, the relaxation and meditation benefit from hot springs and steams.

    However, as the time passes and with the innovation happening in the world, human’s carelessness made a big impact that caused a big damage to our water bodies. One of the math reason is the improper waste disposal. According to the World Bank Organization, worldwide waste rates are rising. In 2012, on the average, every person has a foot print of 1.2 kg of trash per day. With the rapid growth and increase of population and urbanization, we are expecting to rise the weight of trash to rise by 2.2 billion tons by 2025.

    Recently in August 2018, a southwest monsoon hit the Philippines that brought strong winds and heavy rains and a video went viral on social media. It was the notorious Manila Bay and main road of Roxas Boulevard occupied with flood filled with trash. The main cause of this are the irresponsible citizens and the squatters around the area.

    With these arising problems, it will be beneficial to all to have seminars about proper segregation, proper waste disposal, and reducing and recycling wastes. Our website will be a way to let other people initiate things to do to prevent more damage in our bodies of water specially our main target, the Manila Bay. Simple instructions to do like proper waste segregation, recycling of trashes, for example, disposable water bottles should be kept in one place and bring it to our headquarters for it to be recycled.

    We will also be selling merchandises made with natural resources like bamboos and other indigenous materials from our country. We will be avoiding using metal and steel for our production of our own products because it is also a loss for our nature because with the arising demand of metal straws and the likes, the mine production will rise and it will damage our mountains and hills.

    Just like other Non-Government Organizations (NGO’s), we will be having seminars and projects on proper ways of how to take care our environment specially the bodies of water in the Philippines. We will be having a cleanup for a cause that will attract other citizens not only those in the area of Manila to join our movement.

    Our organization will be providing trash cans around the Roxas Boulevard for people to use and stop them from throwing near the bay. We will also encourage others to be part and volunteer for our organization and make our goal to happen as soon as possible and if it’s possible, our movement will not be only in the Philippines but also around the whole world.
